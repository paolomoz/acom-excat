## Additional Content

This page demonstrates the **Hero Marquee** block in action. The block creates a stunning split-layout hero section perfect for:

- Product launches
- Campaign landing pages
- Feature highlights
- Marketing content

The block is fully responsive and will stack vertically on mobile devices for optimal viewing.

### Key Features

- 50/50 split layout on desktop
- Full-height background image
- Customizable content (logo, heading, body, CTA)
- Dark and light variants
- Responsive design
- SEO-friendly structure
